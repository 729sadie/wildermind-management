Step 1: Find Your Niche and Validate Demand

Why It Matters

A focused niche ensures your automation services hit high-demand markets.

Actions

  1. Research Trends:
    • Study AI marketing trends (e.g., chatbots, programmatic ads) via HubSpot or X posts.
    • Identify growth areas (e.g., e-commerce, SaaS).
  2. Choose a Niche:
    • Target industries with big budgets (e.g., retail, fitness).
    • Solve pain points (e.g., low lead conversion).
  3. Validate:
    • Survey SMBs on X or LinkedIn.
    • Offer a $500 pilot (e.g., AI chatbot setup).

Deliverable: A Niche Report with target industries and pain points.

Step 2: Set Up Your Agency

Why It Matters

A legal, professional setup builds trust and scalability.

Actions

  1. Register:
    • Choose a name (e.g., “AImpact Agency”).
    • Form an LLC (consult a lawyer).
  2. Get Tools:
    • Secure a domain (e.g., Namecheap).
    • Open a business bank account.
  3. Comply:
    • Get GDPR/CCPA certifications for data-heavy automation.
    • Use QuickBooks for finances.

Deliverable: A registered agency with domain and banking.
